PREPARATION #1

RECOMMENDATION: Suspend immigration enforcement within disaster zones, including operations at Customs and Border Patrol check points, to ensure people move safe safely for evacuation and re-entry, to access resources, and to recovery job sites.

PROPOSED IMPLEMENTERS: United States Customs and Border Patrol

—   RATIONALE:

After Harvey, there was wide-spread disappointment and frustration with copious FEMA denials. What many service providers, case managers, and disaster victims learned, however, is that denial and appeal is a normal part of the process. Training from FEMA on the application and appeals process would greatly increase the efficiency of disaster case managers, allowing them to work with more clients in a timely way and garner all resources they are eligible for, and increase resilience.
